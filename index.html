Recipes

Hashbrowns

Ingredients

  • 2 Russet Potatoes, medium, shredded
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup parmesan, shredded
  • 3 Tbsp chives
  • 2 Tbsp all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp chile powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  1. Using your new Peel, peel your potatoes.
  2. Grate the potatoes into some cool water while you heat the oil in your pan.
  3. Drain out all the water, squeezing the potatoes to remove all the excess.
  4. Add all of the remaining ingredients and mix to combine.
  5. When your oil is hot, begin to form your patties. Gather your desired amount of mixture and squeeze to remove any water that has built up. Flatten slightly and carefully place into the oil.
  6. Once the bottom side is golden brown, flip and cook until fully golden and crisp. Drain on a paper towel lined plate.
  7. Enjoy!

Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 6 to 7 cups thinly sliced peeled tart apples
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Pastry for double-crust pie (9 inches)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large egg white
  1. In a small bowl, combine the sugars, flour and spices; set aside. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat.
  2. Line a 9-in. pie plate with bottom crust; trim even with edge. Fill with apple mixture; dot with butter. Roll remaining crust to fit top of pie; place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edges. Cut slits in crust.
  3. Beat egg white until foamy; brush over crust. Sprinkle with sugar. Cover edges loosely with foil.
  4. Bake at 375Â° for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly, 20-25 minutes longer. Cool on a wire rack.
